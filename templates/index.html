Recognize and Resist

A simple web application designed to facilitate the detection of cyberbullying instances on Instagram platform. Utilizing a Multilingual Transformer-Based Framework and Bidirectional LSTM with Attention Mechanism, our application aims to provide a solution for identifying potential cyberbullying occurrences.

This is in partial fulfillment of "Cultural Contexts of Cyberbullying on Instagram: A Multilingual Transformer-Based Framework and Bidirectional LSTM with Attention Mechanism" , a thesis of three students from the Technological Institute of the Philippines - Quezon City: Joshua Avena, Svepllana Celeste and Lance Evangelista.
